The Challenge

Digital brand, no physical presence

UNCOMN had strong digital branding but zero standardized print materials. Every department was creating their own collateral with inconsistent fonts, colors, and messaging. Career fairs and client meetings suffered from a fragmented visual identity.

The Solution

A scalable print system that matches the web

I designed and produced a complete suite of print materials — retractable banners, tabletop signage, branded one-pager templates, and recruitment flyers. Every piece extended the digital brand system into physical touchpoints with print-ready precision.

Process

How I got there

01 — Align

Met with stakeholders to understand messaging requirements, audience contexts, and event-specific constraints.

02 — Design

Created layouts in Illustrator and InDesign with brand-consistent typography, color, and visual hierarchy.

03 — Proof

Verified print dimensions, bleed zones, and vendor compatibility. Ran proofs for color accuracy.

04 — Deliver

Exported press-ready PDFs and optimized digital versions for email distribution and internal use.
