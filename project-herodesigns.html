The Challenge

First impressions that don't convert

Most websites lose visitors in the first 3 seconds. Across multiple client and concept projects, I was tasked with designing hero sections that immediately communicate brand value, establish emotional tone, and drive action.

The Solution

Scroll-stopping design with strategic intent

Each hero was built with scroll-based engagement triggers, brand-specific storytelling, emotional headlines, and strategically placed CTAs. Responsive behavior was tested across breakpoints to ensure the impact translates to every screen size.

Process

How I got there

01 — Research

Analyzed competitor hero sections and industry benchmarks for engagement and scroll depth.

02 — Concept

Explored multiple directions per brand — testing different headline weights, imagery styles, and CTA positions.

03 — Refine

Iterated based on feedback loops, narrowing to the strongest visual + messaging combination.

04 — Deliver

Produced final responsive mockups with animation specifications and developer handoff documentation.
