Print and Product Design

Transforming a corporate website into a modern, high-converting experience rooted in data, usability, and bold design.

Overview

UNCOMN’s previous website was outdated and difficult to navigate, lacking both SEO best practices and conversion-driven design. I led a full-scale UX and content overhaul that made the experience more strategic, human-centered, and measurable.

Project Scope

A full-site redesign including sitemap restructuring, updated service pages, visual hierarchy improvements, form optimization, GA4 + Consent Mode implementation, Elementor rebuild, and content writing to better reflect UNCOMN’s voice and offerings.

My Process

Step 1: Conducted stakeholder interviews and a competitive UX audit
Step 2: Created wireframes and UI components focused on lead generation
Step 3: Built the site using Elementor Pro with responsive best practices
Step 4: Integrated analytics (GA4, GTM, A/B testing, Hotjar) and tested cross-browser
